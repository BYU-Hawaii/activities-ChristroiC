Introduction to Tic Tac Toe

Tic Tac Toe is a two-player game where the goal is to be the first to get three of your marks (X or O) in a row, column, or diagonal. Players take turns placing their marks on a 3x3 grid.

Basic Strategies

  • Center First: Always try to take the center if it's available. It gives the maximum number of ways to win.
  • Corner Play: If the center is taken, try to take one of the corners.
  • Block Opponent: Always look for the opponent's two marks in a row and block them.
  • Fork: Create opportunities where you have two ways to win simultaneously.

Advanced Strategies

  • Force Moves: Position your marks to force your opponent into a specific move, setting up your win.

  • Minimax Algorithm: Introduce the concept of the Minimax algorithm, where players minimize the possible loss for a worst-case scenario.

Mathematical Concepts

  • Possible Outcomes: Discuss the number of possible moves and the concept of permutations and combinations.
  • Probability: Calculate the probability of winning from different positions on the board.
  • Symmetry: Discuss the symmetry of the board and how it reduces the number of unique positions.
