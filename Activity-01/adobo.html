Pork Adobo Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs pork belly or shoulder, cut into cubes
  • 1 cup soy sauce
  • 1 cup vinegar
  • 6 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 teaspoon peppercorns
  • 1/2 cup water

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, combine pork, soy sauce, and garlic. Marinate for 30 minutes.
  2. In a pot, heat oil over medium heat. Add onions and cook until translucent.
  3. Add marinated pork and cook until browned.
  4. Pour vinegar into the pot and let it simmer for 5 minutes.
  5. Add water, bay leaf, and peppercorns. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 30-40 minutes or until pork is tender.
  6. Serve hot with rice. Enjoy your Pork Adobo!
